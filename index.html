Our Leader

Meet Our MD/CEO

The company is managed by a Team of Well experienced and focused Board and Management and lead by Abdulah, Bashir Korede -Bsc Hon Econs, Msc, PhD, FCA, ACITN,CFIP

He has over 35years experience in the private sector and was the Finance Director/Company Secretary of CAP Plc –a Subsidiary of UACN PLC and Technical licensee of Akzo Nobel, the owners of Dulux – the world no1 decorative paint before becoming the Chairman/Managing Director of Sekibat Nig Ltd.

He has varied experiences in the operations of companies in Supply Chain and Distribution Management, Projects and Real Estates Development and Management, Manufacturing, Oil and Gas, Government Agencies and Professional Service sectors.

He is also the Principal Consultant in the firm of Messrs B K Abdulah & Co – a firm of Chartered Accountants, Tax and Forensic Auditors. With an Msc and Phd in Forensic Accounting and Investigation, he is a member of the International Institute of certified Forensic Investigation Professionals (CFIP), USA, Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria ( ICAN) and member of the Chartered Institute of Taxation of Nigeria.

He has attended many Seminars, Workshop, Professional, Project Management, Marketing and Procurement and Leadership Development courses at Lagos Business School and other Institutions in Nigeria and abroad.
